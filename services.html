Professional Investigation Services

Comprehensive forensic solutions backed by expertise, ethics, and evidence-based methodologies

Litigation Support & Witness Location

What's Delivered: Expert witness testimony, evidence preservation protocols, document discovery support, deposition preparation, and chain-of-custody documentation. Witness interviews, statement verification, and location services for critical testimonies.

Typical Clients: Trial attorneys, corporate counsel, insurance companies, and legal departments requiring court-ready evidence and expert testimony support.

Expected Deliverables: Comprehensive investigation reports, sworn affidavits, expert witness testimony, evidence logs with chain-of-custody documentation, and court-admissible findings presentations.

Due Diligence & Background Investigations

What's Delivered: Comprehensive background verification, corporate intelligence gathering, financial history analysis, reputation assessment, and risk evaluation for business transactions, employment decisions, or partnership evaluations.

Typical Clients: Investment firms, corporate executives, legal teams, and individuals requiring thorough vetting of business partners, potential employees, or transaction counterparts.

Expected Deliverables: Detailed background reports, risk assessments, corporate structure analysis, financial health evaluations, and verified credential confirmations.

Corporate/Internal Investigations & Compliance Support

What's Delivered: Internal fraud investigations, employee misconduct inquiries, regulatory compliance audits, workplace incident analysis, and corporate policy violation investigations.

Typical Clients: Corporate legal departments, human resources teams, compliance officers, and executive management requiring objective internal investigations.

Expected Deliverables: Confidential investigation reports, policy recommendation documents, compliance gap analyses, and actionable remediation strategies.

Digital Forensics & eDiscovery Support

What's Delivered: Electronic evidence recovery, data preservation protocols, digital device analysis, email and communication recovery, metadata analysis, and forensic imaging services.

Typical Clients: Law firms, corporate IT departments, legal teams handling intellectual property cases, and organizations responding to regulatory investigations.

Expected Deliverables: Forensic analysis reports, recovered digital evidence, chain-of-custody documentation, expert witness testimony on digital findings, and court-ready evidence presentations.

Asset & Financial Investigations

What's Delivered: Hidden asset location, financial transaction tracing, business valuation support, banking relationship analysis, and asset recovery coordination with legal counsel.

Typical Clients: Divorce attorneys, collection agencies, creditors, bankruptcy trustees, and legal teams pursuing asset recovery in civil litigation.

Expected Deliverables: Asset discovery reports, financial flow analysis, banking records summaries, valuation support documentation, and recovery strategy recommendations.

Crisis Response & Skip Tracing

What's Delivered: Emergency location services, time-sensitive investigations, missing person searches, debtor location, witness finding, and rapid evidence preservation protocols.

Typical Clients: Legal teams with urgent deadlines, process servers, collection attorneys, and organizations requiring immediate location services.

Expected Deliverables: Rapid location reports, current address verification, contact information updates, service of process coordination, and urgent investigation findings.

Frequently Asked Questions

We maintain strict confidentiality protocols including signed non-disclosure agreements, secure communication channels, encrypted data storage, and need-to-know access controls. All team members undergo confidentiality training and background verification. Work product is protected by professional privilege where applicable.

All investigations strictly comply with federal and state laws. We use lawful discovery methods, public records research, consensual interviews, and court-authorized processes. We never engage in illegal activities such as hacking, identity theft, unauthorized surveillance, or false pretenses. All evidence gathering follows proper legal procedures to ensure admissibility.

We follow strict chain-of-custody protocols including detailed evidence logs, secure storage procedures, time-stamped documentation, and proper handling procedures. All evidence collection methods are documented, and our experts are qualified to provide testimony regarding evidence authenticity and collection methodology.

Our team includes licensed private investigators, certified fraud examiners, forensic accountants, former law enforcement professionals, and court-qualified expert witnesses. All investigators maintain current professional licenses, continuing education requirements, and liability insurance. Many hold advanced degrees and specialized certifications in their areas of expertise.

We understand the time-sensitive nature of many investigations. Initial consultation can be scheduled within 24 hours, and evidence preservation measures can begin immediately upon engagement. For urgent matters requiring asset preservation or time-sensitive evidence collection, we provide rapid response services with 24-48 hour case initiation.
