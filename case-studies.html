Multi-Million Dollar Asset Recovery in Complex Divorce

Asset Recovery 6-Week Investigation

Challenge

High-net-worth divorce case where the opposing party claimed minimal assets despite a history of substantial income. Client's attorney suspected hidden wealth in offshore accounts and complex corporate structures. Traditional discovery was yielding incomplete financial disclosure, and there was concern about potential asset dissipation.

Approach

Deployed multi-disciplinary team including forensic accountant, private investigator, and international asset specialist. Conducted expedited UCC searches revealing collateralized assets, analyzed five years of banking records through formal discovery, traced corporate ownership chains across three jurisdictions, and coordinated with local counsel in offshore financial centers for legal discovery requests.

Key Evidence & Deliverables

  • Banking records showing systematic transfers to nominee accounts
  • Corporate filings revealing beneficial ownership of shell companies
  • Real estate records documenting properties held through trusts
  • Investment account statements showing substantial portfolio values
  • Expert witness testimony on asset tracing methodology

Outcome & Impact

Result: Discovered $4.2 million in previously undisclosed assets including offshore investments, domestic real estate holdings, and business interests. Evidence led to successful court order for asset preservation and favorable settlement negotiation.

Client Impact: Achieved equitable distribution of marital assets, prevented permanent loss of substantial wealth, and provided comprehensive documentation for ongoing enforcement actions.

Corporate Fraud Investigation & Recovery

Corporate Investigation 8-Week Investigation

Challenge

Manufacturing company discovered significant inventory shortages and unexplained financial discrepancies suggesting internal fraud. Management needed to identify responsible parties, quantify losses, and develop evidence for potential criminal prosecution and civil recovery. Investigation required discretion to prevent evidence destruction and maintain business operations.

Approach

Conducted covert digital forensics on company systems, analyzed three years of financial records and inventory tracking data, performed discrete employee interviews and background checks, coordinated with law enforcement for potential criminal referral, and implemented document preservation protocols to secure evidence integrity.

Key Evidence & Deliverables

  • Electronic evidence of unauthorized system access and data manipulation
  • Financial analysis revealing systematic inventory diversion patterns
  • Documentation of fraudulent vendor payments and fictitious invoices
  • Employee banking records showing unexplained deposits
  • Chain-of-custody documentation for all digital and physical evidence

Outcome & Impact

Result: Identified $1.8 million in losses due to inventory theft and fraudulent payments by two employees working in collusion. Evidence package supported successful criminal prosecution and civil judgment for full restitution.

Client Impact: Recovered substantial losses through insurance claims and civil litigation, implemented improved internal controls, and maintained business relationships through discrete investigation process.

Commercial Litigation Evidence Development

Litigation Support 12-Week Investigation

Challenge

Technology company facing breach of contract lawsuit needed to demonstrate damages and establish timeline of events. Opposing party claimed no financial harm and disputed key factual allegations. Case required expert analysis of complex business records and technical data to support client's position in high-stakes commercial litigation.

Approach

Coordinated comprehensive document discovery across multiple data sources, conducted forensic analysis of computer systems and electronic communications, engaged industry experts for technical and financial analysis, developed timeline reconstruction using metadata and communication records, and prepared expert witnesses for deposition and trial testimony.

Key Evidence & Deliverables

  • Comprehensive timeline reconstruction with supporting documentation
  • Financial analysis quantifying client's monetary damages
  • Electronic evidence of opposing party's knowledge and intent
  • Expert witness reports on technical and financial issues
  • Court-ready evidence presentations and demonstrative exhibits

Outcome & Impact

Result: Developed compelling evidence package supporting $3.4 million in provable damages. Expert testimony withstood opposing counsel's challenges during depositions and provided foundation for successful motion for summary judgment.

Client Impact: Achieved favorable settlement exceeding client's expectations, avoided lengthy trial costs, and established precedent for future contract enforcement with business partners.
